## What It Is
An underwater exploration game with no combat and no fail state — you are a lone diver descending through ocean ecosystems teeming with hundreds of species. You swim, glide, and ride currents; the world answers with schooling fish, shifting light, and the slow reveal of ancient ruins. The story is wordless, told entirely through movement and the places you pass into. Made by the art director of *Journey* — meditative, painterly, built around awe rather than challenge.
