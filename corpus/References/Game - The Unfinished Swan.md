## What It Is
A first-person fairy tale that begins in a completely white world — you throw blobs of black paint to reveal the environment as you go. A boy named Monroe chases an unfinished swan from his late mother's painting into the unfinished kingdom of an absent king. Each chapter overturns the previous mechanic (paint, then light and shadow, then vines that grow where you spray water, then building blocks).
