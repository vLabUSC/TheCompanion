## Summary

You arrive expecting to play through a heist adventure — but the game isn't ready. Instead of starring in the heist, you're pulled backstage and asked to help run it for someone else. A frantic narrator (voiced by Simon Amstell) guides you through behind-the-scenes tasks: operating stage lights, releasing a tiger on cue, triggering sound effects. The "real" game is always happening just on the other side of the walls, and you're the stagehand keeping it going.

The joke — and the insight — is that the backstage *is* the game. There is no actual heist happening on the other side. The player cannot cross the world's boundary.
