## What It Is
A real-time simulation of a six-hour transatlantic flight, played from a coach window seat. There is no goal — you stow your carry-on, flip through the magazine, watch the safety video, fight the seatback entertainment system, doze, and wait out the turbulence. The whole game is the texture of confinement and tedium rendered with obsessive fidelity. A dry, deadpan piece about a mundane space most people share — interactive art made from boredom.
