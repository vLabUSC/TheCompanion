**Creators:** David Lynch and Mark Frost | **Year:** 1990–1991 (series), 2017 (The Return) | **Network:** ABC / Showtime

Twin Peaks began as a murder mystery set in a small Pacific Northwest logging town, then expanded into a cosmology involving parallel dimensions, inhabiting spirits, and the Black Lodge — a red-curtained realm operating on its own dream logic.

The Black Lodge is a world with a crossable boundary — characters enter and return changed, or don't return at all.
