**Developer:** Arvi Teikari (Hempuli) · **Year:** 2019 · **Platform:** PC, Switch
**→ [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/736260/Baba_Is_You/)**

Puzzle game in which the rules of the world are visible on screen as word blocks — "Baba is You," "Wall is Stop," "Flag is Win." Push the blocks to rewrite the rules, and the world changes accordingly. Move "Win" onto a different object, make walls passable, turn yourself into a rock.
