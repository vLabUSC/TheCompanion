
*Yertle the Turtle* is a children's book by Dr. Seuss, published in 1958.

[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yertle_the_Turtle_and_Other_Stories)

## Summary

Yertle is the king of a small pond. Dissatisfied with his view, he commands the other turtles to stack themselves beneath him so he can sit higher and see — and rule — more. The stack grows taller and taller as Yertle demands ever greater dominion. At the bottom of the stack, a plain little turtle named Mack complains about the pain and hunger the turtles are suffering. Yertle ignores him. Finally, Mack burps — and the whole stack topples. Yertle falls into the mud, and the turtles of the pond are free.
