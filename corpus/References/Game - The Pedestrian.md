## What It Is
A puzzle platformer where you play as the figure from a pedestrian-crossing sign, hopping between panels of public signage by rearranging them. The world is the visual furniture of city life — restroom icons, fire-exit diagrams, restaurant menus — re-seen as a place you can move through.
