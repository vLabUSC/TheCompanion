
*World of Tomorrow* is an animated short film by Don Hertzfeldt, released in 2015. It won the Grand Jury Prize at Sundance and was nominated for an Academy Award.

https://vimeo.com/405922332?share=copy&fl=sv&fe=ci

## Summary

A little girl named Emily is contacted by a third-generation clone of herself from the far future. The clone takes Emily on a tour through time, showing her memories of love, loss, the moon, dying stars, and digital afterlives. 

The film moves through a series of strange, minimal worlds — each one a bounded space defined by a single memory or idea. The boundary-crossing happens constantly: Emily is pulled from one world into another.
