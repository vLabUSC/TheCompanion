**Year:** 2013
**Platform:** PC
**Fame tier:** successful indie

A first-person exploration game with no combat, no puzzle-solving — just a house to explore and a story to reconstruct. You return to your family's home to find it empty. Every room is a scene left mid-action. The story of what happened is assembled entirely through objects, notes, and environmental detail.
