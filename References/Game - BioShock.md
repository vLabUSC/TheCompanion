## What It Is
A first-person shooter set in Rapture — an underwater city built around Ayn Rand's philosophy of radical individualism. By the time you arrive, the city has collapsed. You reconstruct what happened through the environment: audio diaries, advertising, architecture, and the behavior of what's left.
