**Developer:** Minority | **Year:** 2012 | **Platform:** PC, console
**→ [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/227080/Papo_Yo/)**

## What It Is
A puzzle-platformer set in a favela. Your companion is a giant monster. The monster is helpful when calm, but when he eats frogs, he becomes violent and dangerous. The game is autobiographical allegory: the developer's father was an alcoholic.
