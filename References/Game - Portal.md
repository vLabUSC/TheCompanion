## What It Is
A first-person puzzle game. You have a portal gun that creates linked openings in surfaces. The world is a testing facility. GLaDOS watches.

As the player gains control of the portals, the logic is consistent.
- 0:20 - trapped
- 1:05 - portals given
- 3:09 - gun creates blue portal
- 13:20 - gun creates orange portal as well
