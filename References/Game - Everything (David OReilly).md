## What It Is
You can become anything — an animal, a rock, a galaxy, a blade of grass. Scale is infinite in both directions. 

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HdJk8ROpuEo
## Notes for Class
Useful for Week 5 (physical world / observer's position). The question it raises: if you can be anything, is there a "world" at all, or just a collection of perspectives?
