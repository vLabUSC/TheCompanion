**Director:** Denis Villeneuve | **Years:** 2021, 2024
*Based on the novel by Frank Herbert (1965)*

## What It Is
The story of Paul Atreides and the desert planet Arrakis — the only source of spice, the most valuable substance in the universe. The world is built from a single ecological fact outward: there is almost no water on Arrakis. Everything else follows from that.
