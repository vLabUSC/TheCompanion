## What It Is
A short, text-heavy interactive narrative about a breakup. Nick has just been left by Madison after four years; you enter his apartment in the aftermath and move through his memories of the relationship. You also step into neighbors' apartments — small interactive vignettes, each its own study of love and isolation. There is little to *do* in the action sense; the game is environmental storytelling and reading, intimate and introspective, built to sit with loss rather than resolve it.
