**Director:** Bong Joon-ho | **Year:** 2013 | **Runtime:** 126 min
*Based on the French graphic novel Le Transperceneige*

After a climate catastrophe freezes the Earth, all surviving humanity lives on a perpetually moving train circling the globe. The train is the world — its class hierarchy enforced by position in the cars.

The train is a closed system with enforced limits. Moving forward through the cars is the only direction the world allows, and the engine at the front is the uncrossable horizon.
