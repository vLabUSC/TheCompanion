**Director:** Henry Selick | **Year:** 2009 | **Runtime:** 100 min
*Based on the novella by Neil Gaiman*

Coraline discovers a small door in her new house that leads to an "Other World" — a version of her life in which everything is better and her "Other Mother" is attentive and loving. 

The Other World is a finite world built as a lure.  The small door is the crossable boundary, and the question of whether Coraline can return through it drives the film.
