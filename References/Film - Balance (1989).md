**Directors:** Christoph & Wolfgang Lauenstein · **Year:** 1989 · **Runtime:** 7 min
Academy Award, Best Animated Short (1990)

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SFH6yRYOI54

Five identical men stand on a small platform floating in empty space. When any one of them moves, the platform tilts — the others must shift to compensate. They fish over the edge. One pulls up a heavy box that plays music. The struggle to possess it destroys the balance they all depend on, making it a moral tale.
