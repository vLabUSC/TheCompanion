**Year:** 2015
**Platform:** PC, iOS
**Fame tier:** successful indie

A database investigation game with no physical world to explore. The player searches a police archive of video clips — interviews recorded across multiple sessions — by typing keywords. Each search surfaces fragments; contradictions accumulate. The full story emerges  through the player's own inference.
