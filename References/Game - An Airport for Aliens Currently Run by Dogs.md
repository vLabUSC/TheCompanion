## What It Is
A first-person open-world comedy adventure set across airports staffed entirely by dogs. You and your fiancée are the last two humans alive, so you travel — through terminals with functioning flight timetables — chatting with stock-photo dogs, solving their problems, translating alien languages, and catching connections to see her. A walking simulator built on absurdist writing and charm rather than challenge: surreal, warm, and quietly about long-distance love.
