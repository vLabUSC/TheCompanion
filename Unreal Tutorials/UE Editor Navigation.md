
- **Right-click + drag** — Look around (free look)
- **Right-click + WASD** — Fly through the scene (hold right-click to enable movement keys)
- **Right-click + Q/E** — Fly down / fly up
- **Mouse scroll wheel** — Zoom in and out
- **Alt + left-click + drag** — Orbit around a point
- **Alt + right-click + drag** — Dolly (zoom toward/away from a point)
- **Alt + middle-click + drag** — Pan the view
- **F** — Focus/frame the selected object (centers it in the viewport)
- **Mouse scroll wheel (hold) + drag** — Pan the view (alternative to Alt + middle-click)
- **Right-click + mouse scroll wheel** — Change fly speed while navigating

Look at the Outliner.  It lists all the "actors" in the scene.
